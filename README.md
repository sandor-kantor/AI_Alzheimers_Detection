# Alzheimer's disease detection by AI based on brain scan images

An advanced artificial intelligence (AI) method will be developed to detect Alzheimer’s disease through brain scan images. By analysing intricate patterns within brain structures and pinpointing subtle changes in specific regions, this method will offer early and accurate indications of the disease. This approach will empower clinicians in diagnosing Alzheimer’s disease and devising the most suitable treatment plan by providing precise data-driven insights gleaned from imaging studies. star
